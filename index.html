ABOUT

Mohanlal Ravi stands out as an artist who charts his own course in the music world and manages all production processes single-handedly. Discovering his interest in music during his childhood, he has been honing his skills as a producer for about 6-7 years. In his music career, drawing inspiration from international rap artists like Juice WRLD, Lil Uzi Vert, and Young Thug, as well as local artists such as Khontkar, Lil Zey, and BEGE, he has developed his unique sound in the instrumental trap genre.
